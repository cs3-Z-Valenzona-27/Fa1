Soccer
  • Soccer is a popular team sport played between two teams, each consisting of 11 players.
  • The primary objective of the game is to score goals by getting the ball into the opposing team's net, located at opposite ends of the field.
  • Soccer is known for its continuous, fluid gameplay, with few stoppages. Players use their feet, head, or other parts of their body (except their hands and arms) to control and pass the ball.
  • It is one of the most widely followed and participated sports in the world, with a massive fan base and professional leagues in numerous countries.
